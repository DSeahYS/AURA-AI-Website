About AURA AI

AURA AI represents a breakthrough in space operations technology. Our advanced AI system has been specifically trained on extensive EVA (Extravehicular Activity) procedure documentation to provide astronauts and mission control with instant, accurate procedural guidance.

Unlike general-purpose AI models, AURA AI understands the unique context of space operations, safety protocols, and the critical nature of EVA procedures. This specialized training enables AURA to provide responses that are not only accurate but also contextually appropriate for space mission scenarios.
